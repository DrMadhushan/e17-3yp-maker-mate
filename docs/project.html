About Maker Mate Project

Maker Space is the new buiding of the Department of Computer Engineeering of University Peradeniya. To make the Maker Space more convenient for it's user the department had below given requirements. Our goal is to satisfy them.Therfore, our product is a client based, customized product.

Need analysis

Inventory Management
A System to manage the Maker Space inventory online is required. The tools and equipments in the labotary need to be managed and monitored in a convenient and efficient way.
Apply online
The students must have the ability to apply fot the inventory that they need fo thier project work online. Also they should be able to submit thier designs and get the approval from the department.
A navigator
The navigator is to guide the users in the maker space. This must help them to save thier time and work efficiently. And also it should show the route to their pickup locker for specific users.
Online reservation
The users must be able to reserve a place in maker space for thier project and experiment work in order to use the resources in the maker space. This should prevent the clashes and should inform the useres about thier time allocations.
